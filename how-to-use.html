Getting Started

What is NexCore Labs?

NexCore Labs is a student-driven digital solution platform designed for student projects across universities and colleges in Oman. We transform your ideas into professional, deployable page that showcase your team's innovation and creativity.

Key Benefits:

Fast project deployment • Professional page presence • Search engine optimization • Community visibility

Who Can Use NexCore Labs?

Our platform is open to students at any university or college in Oman. Whether you're working on a research initiative, tech startup, creative venture, or innovation project, NexCore Labs provides the infrastructure and expertise to bring your vision online with a page that showcases your project.

Quick Start in 3 Steps

  1. Visit the Hub
    Navigate to the NexCore Labs Hub to explore existing projects and understand our capabilities.
  2. Sign Up Your Team
    Click "Access the Core" and register your project details through our secure signup form.
  3. Work with Our Team
    We will collaborate with you to create a professional page tailored to your project's needs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow the guide that matches your situation to get your project live on NexCore.

Free access for all SQU affiliates. Your SQU Google account is automatically recognised — no payment required at any stage. Students from other Oman universities can access the platform via the paid plans.
  1. Sign in with your SQU Google Account Go to auth.html and click Continue with Google. Use your official SQU email (e.g. s000000@student.squ.edu.om).
  2. Get Instant Access & Land on Your Dashboard SQU emails are recognised automatically. Once signed in you are taken directly to your dashboard — no waiting, no payment, no extra steps.
  3. Manage Your Project From your dashboard you can view your project card, update details, and track your project's visibility on the NexCore Hub.
Non-SQU users require a one-time access fee. This includes students from other Oman universities and external users. All prices are in Omani Rial (OMR). Visit the Pricing page to build your plan.
  1. Visit the Pricing Page Go to pricing.html. Every order includes Lifetime Access (0.950 OMR) — your project never expires on NexCore.
  2. Choose Your Add-ons & Support Duration Optionally add Priority Review (0.300 OMR — published within 24 hours) or a support duration package (1 week → 1 year). The live price bar updates as you select.
  3. Fill in the Order Form Enter your full name, Google account email (used to link your NexCore account), and optionally your WhatsApp number for faster communication.
  4. Choose a Payment Method Select either:
    • Bank Transfer — Transfer to our Bank Muscat account. Verified within ~48 hours.
    • PayPal — Pay in USD (1 OMR = 2.60 USD). Activation is instant. A small PayPal processing fee is added.
  5. Complete Payment & Submit Follow the on-screen instructions for your chosen method and click Submit Order. You will receive a confirmation once your order is registered.
  6. Wait for Account Activation Bank Transfer orders are activated within ~48 hours after payment is confirmed. PayPal orders are activated instantly. You will receive an email notification.
  7. Sign in & Land on Your Dashboard Once activated, go to auth.html and sign in with the Google account you provided in the order. You will be taken directly to your dashboard.
  8. Manage Your Project From your dashboard you can view your project card, update details, and track your project's visibility on the NexCore Hub.

Accessing the Hub

What is the Hub?

The NexCore Labs Hub (hub.html) is the project management and exploration center. It showcases all active projects, displays project details, and provides team information.

How to Access the Hub

  1. From the Navigation Menu
    Click the dots menu (⋯⋯) in the top-right corner and select "Hub".
  2. Using the "Open Hub" Button
    On the homepage hero section, click the "Open Hub" button.
  3. Direct URL Access
    Navigate directly to hub.html

Hub Features

Project Listings
Browse all active projects with descriptions, team information, and direct links to project pages.
Secure Signup
