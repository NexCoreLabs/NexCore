Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NexCore Labs, our services, and how to get the most out of our platform.

General

What is NexCore Labs?

NexCore Labs is a student-run digital solutions platform at Sultan Qaboos University, specializing in building pages and digital experiences for student-led projects. We focus on speed, quality, and collaboration to bring your ideas to life online.

Who can use NexCore Labs?

NexCore Labs serves Sultan Qaboos University students and affiliated groups, including research teams, student startups, clubs, and creative projects. If you're an SQU student working on a project, we can help you get online quickly and professionally.

When was NexCore Labs founded?

NexCore Labs was founded in Sep 23, 2025 by a dedicated team of SQU students passionate about web development and innovation. We're a young, energetic team committed to helping student projects shine online.

How do I get started with NexCore Labs?

Getting started is simple! Sign up your project through our Hub, fill in your project details, and our team will contact you within 48 hours to discuss your needs and timeline. Check out our How to Use guide for more details.

What's the difference between NexCore Labs and other web development services?

NexCore Labs 100% led by Omani team and it's uniquely focused on student projects at SQU. We understand the constraints of student teams — limited budgets, learning objectives, and tight timelines. Our team is dedicated to delivering professional results while mentoring the next generation of developers. We prioritize speed, quality, and affordability.

Technical

What technologies do you use?

Our tech stack includes:
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+
  • Backend: Supabase
  • Tools: GitHub, VS Code, Git
  • Performance: PageSpeed optimization
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Search Console(SEO)

Is NexCore Labs website Progressive Web App (PWA) compatible?

Yes! NexCore Labs is built as Progressive Web Apps. This means users can install the site on their devices like a native app, enjoy offline functionality, and get push notifications. It's a modern, user-friendly approach that boosts engagement.

How is performance optimized?

We implement multiple performance optimizations:
  • Image optimization and lazy loading
  • WebP format for modern browsers
  • CSS and JavaScript minification
  • Caching strategies via service workers
  • Regular performance audits with PageSpeed Insights

What about SEO?

All our pages are SEO optimized from day one:
  • Proper meta tags and descriptions
  • Semantic HTML structure
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Fast loading times (Core Web Vitals)
  • XML sitemaps and robots.txt
  • Google Search Console integration
  • Schema markup for rich snippets

Can I edit the code myself after launch?

Yes! We provide you with full access to your project's source code on GitHub. If you have developers on your team, they can make updates themselves (with our guidance). We also offer ongoing support hours for content and feature updates.
