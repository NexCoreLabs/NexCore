Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 3, 2026

What data we collect

  • Usage data (pageviews, timestamps) via Google Analytics — only with your explicit consent.
  • Form input you voluntarily submit (name, email, message) when contacting us.
  • Technical logs related to hosting and service operation (server logs).

Why we collect data

We use analytics to understand how the site is used so we can improve performance, accessibility, and user experience. Contact form data is used solely to respond to your request.

Legal basis

Where required under EU law, we rely on your consent as the legal basis for collecting analytics data. You can accept or reject analytics cookies via the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Third-party processors

  • Google LLC — Google Analytics 4 (Measurement ID: G-PYZB5L2R8W). GA is only loaded if you accept analytics cookies. We enable IP anonymization and disable ad personalization.
  • Hosting provider (Vercel / static CDN) — for serving site content. Hosting logs may contain technical information (IP address, request timestamps) which are processed for security and availability.

Your GDPR rights

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data where we do not have another legal basis to retain it.
  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent for analytics at any time using the cookie banner or by visiting this page and using the button below.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, contact us at nexcorelabs@outlook.com. Please provide enough information to identify the data you want to access or delete.
