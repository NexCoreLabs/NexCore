Pricing Questions

What's included in the setup fee?

The setup includes initial consultation, design creation, development, testing, deployment, and 2 weeks of post-launch support to ensure everything works perfectly.

Can I upgrade plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher plan anytime. We'll credit any remaining balance from your current plan towards the upgrade.

Is there a setup fee?

Yes, we charge a one-time setup fee of $299 to cover initial consultation, design, and deployment. This is waived for annual subscriptions to the Professional and Enterprise plans.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! SQU students receive a 20% discount on all plans. Simply verify your SQU email address during checkout to apply the discount automatically.

What happens after the support period ends?

Your website remains live and accessible. You can continue the monthly subscription for ongoing support, or opt for our pay-as-you-go support model at $49/hour.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual plans include a 30-day refund guarantee. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with our service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For larger enterprise projects, we offer flexible payment plans.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts required. Monthly plans are flexible and can be adjusted or cancelled with 30 days notice. We rely on quality service to keep your business, not contracts.
