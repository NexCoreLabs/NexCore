Pricing Questions

What's included in the setup fee?

The setup includes initial consultation, design creation, development, testing, deployment, and 2-days of post-launch support to ensure everything works perfectly.

Can I upgrade plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher plan anytime. We'll credit any remaining balance from your current plan towards the upgrade.

Is there a setup fee?

Yes, we charge a one-time setup fee of 0.500 OMR to cover initial consultation, design, and deployment. This is waived just for Professional subscription.

What happens after the support period ends?

Your page will remain available and accessible. You can subscribe to a monthly support plan, or choose a pay-as-you-go support model at 0.800 OMR/hour.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, Plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Plans include a 7-day refund guarantee. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with our service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, and bank transfers only.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts required. We rely on quality service to keep your business, not contracts.
